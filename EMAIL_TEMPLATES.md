
### Personalization Tips

1. **Always use first name** in subject and greeting
2. **Include relevant metrics** specific to their account
3. **Segment by plan type** (Starter vs Pro vs Enterprise)
4. **Segment by usage** (active vs inactive)
5. **Segment by lifecycle stage** (trial, paid, churned)

## Email Best Practices

### Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters
- Use emojis sparingly (1 per subject max)
- Include personalization ([First Name])
- Create urgency when appropriate ("3 days left")
- A/B test everything

### Body Content
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences max)
- Use bullet points for scanning
- One clear CTA per email
- Mobile-friendly formatting
- Include social proof when relevant

### Timing
- Welcome: Immediately
- Day 2: 48 hours after signup
- Day 5: Midpoint of trial
- Day 10: 4 days before trial ends
- Monthly: Same day each month
- Billing: 3 days before charge

### Metrics to Track
- Open rate (target: 20-30%)
- Click rate (target: 3-5%)
- Conversion rate (target: varies by email type)
- Unsubscribe rate (keep under 0.5%)
