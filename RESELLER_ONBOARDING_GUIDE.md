## üèÜ Success Tips

### Best Practices

1. **Focus on value, not features**
   - Talk about cost savings (60% reduction in support costs)
   - Emphasize 24/7 availability
   - Show conversion rate improvements (20-30% increase)

2. **Use case studies liberally**
   - Industry-specific examples resonate more
   - Numbers matter: "Reduced response time from 4 hours to 30 seconds"

3. **Offer free trials**
   - 14-day trial removes friction
   - Most convert after seeing results

4. **Start with high-volume businesses**
   - Companies with 100+ support tickets/month see biggest ROI
   - Easier to justify subscription cost

5. **Bundle with other services**
   - Partner with web developers
   - Offer as add-on to marketing services
   - Package with SEO or social media management

### Common Objections & Responses

**"We already have live chat"**
‚Üí "AI chatbots complement live chat by handling simple queries automatically, so your team focuses on complex issues. Studies show 80% of inquiries can be automated."

**"Too expensive"**

**"Our customers prefer talking to humans"**
‚Üí "So do we! But when it's 2 AM and they need help, they prefer instant answers over waiting 10 hours. The chatbot provides instant help, and can seamlessly hand off to your team when needed."

**"We're too small"**
‚Üí "That's perfect! The Starter plan is designed for small businesses at just $29/month. You get 60 conversations/month, which is ideal for growing companies."

**"It won't understand our industry"**
‚Üí "We have industry-specific templates for [their industry]. You train the bot with your FAQs, product info, and policies - it learns your business."
